Department of Artificial Intelligence

Kyungdong University Global Campus

Overview

The Department of Artificial Intelligence at Kyungdong University Global is dedicated to developing talents who can lead the 4th Industrial Revolution. The program focuses on core AI technologies including machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and robotics, emphasizing real-world applications.

Our curriculum bridges theory with practice, fostering students who can contribute to both academia and industry as AI engineers, researchers, and entrepreneurs.

  • Duration of Study: 4 years
  • Medium of Instruction: English (100%)
