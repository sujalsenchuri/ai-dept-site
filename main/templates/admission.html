General Requirements

All applicants must meet the following general requirements to be eligible to apply to KDU Global programs.

English Medium Programs
  • Bachelor of Artificial Intelligence
  • Bachelor of International Business Administration
  • Bachelor of International Hotel Management
  • Bachelor of Smart Computing
  • Bachelor of SNS Media and Film Production

Requirements:

  • Completion of high school or equivalent level of education.
  • No longer than three years of gap after graduating from the most recent formal educational institution.
  • Minimum of IELTS 5.5, TOEFL 530 (CBT 197, iBT 71), CEFR B2, TEPS 601 (New TEPS 327).
  • Excellent academic performance record.
Korean Medium Program

Bachelor of Korean Business Convergence

  • Completion of high school or equivalent level of education.
  • No longer than three years of gap after graduating from the most recent formal educational institution.
  • Minimum of TOPIK Level 3 of Korean proficiency.
  • Excellent academic performance record.

English Medium Programs
  • MA in International Business Administration
  • ME in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • MA in International Hospitality and Tourism Management

Requirements:

  • Completion of undergraduate level of education.
  • Minimum of IELTS 5.5, TOEFL 530 (CBT 197, iBT 71), CEFR B2, TEPS 601 (New TEPS 327).
  • Excellent academic performance record.

English Language for Academic Purposes (EAP)
  • Completion of high school or equivalent level of education.
  • No longer than three years of gap after graduating from the most recent formal educational institution.
  • Passing KDU Global English proficiency test including oral interview.
  • Excellent academic performance record.
Korean Language for Academic Purposes (KAP)
  • Completion of high school or equivalent level of education.
  • No longer than three years of gap after graduating from the most recent formal educational institution.
  • Passing KDU Global Korean proficiency test including oral interview.
  • Excellent academic performance record.

Applicants who would like to transfer to KDU Global or enroll as double-degree or exchange students must contact the Admissions Office for specific requirements and conditions.

All inquiries regarding admissions must be directed to info@kduniv.ac.kr.

Scholarship Opportunities

KDU provides scholarships up to 100% for undergraduate students based on academic performance and merit.
